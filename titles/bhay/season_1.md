A paranormal thriller based on the life of India's foremost paranormal investigator, Gaurav Tiwari. The series explores his pursuit of unexplained phenomena that eventually leads to his mysterious and untimely death. A journalist delves into his legacy to uncover secrets blurring the lines between reality and the supernatural.
